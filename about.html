S. M. Abrar Rajin

S. M. Abrar Rajin is currently pursuing the B.Sc. degree in computer science and engineering with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, North South University, Dhaka, Bangladesh. His current work focuses on developing environmental monitoring systems using graph neural networks and attention-based architectures. He aims to contribute to sustainable development through AI-driven solutions for environmental challenges. His research interests include deep learning, computer vision, and geospatial artificial intelligence.
